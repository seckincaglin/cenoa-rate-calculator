## Key Insights

### Experience Multipliers
- Junior → Mid: +50-80%
- Junior → Senior: 2-3x
- Mid → Senior: +40-60%

### Designer vs Developer Gap
- Developers earn ~30% more than designers at equivalent experience
- Design rates rarely exceed $60/hr even at senior levels
- Lower barrier to entry + competition keeps design rates down

### What Drives Premium Rates
1. AI/ML expertise (+40-60%)
2. Cybersecurity skills (+30-50%)
3. Cloud architecture (+35-55%)
4. Blockchain (+20-40%)
5. DevOps (+25-45%)

### Geographic Arbitrage
- Turkish dev charging US rates: $60-100/hr vs local $20-40/hr
- Same for Latin America, Eastern Europe
- Time zone overlap with US is valuable for LATAM
